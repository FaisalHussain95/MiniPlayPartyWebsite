## Introduction

Welcome to MiniPlayParty. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information**: When you create an account, we collect your username, email address, and authentication credentials.
- **Apple Sign-In / Play Games**: When you use third-party authentication services, we collect information provided by those services.
- **Profile Information**: Display name, avatar/profile picture, and other optional profile details.

### Usage Information
- **Game Activity**: Information about your gameplay, scores, achievements, and game statistics.
- **Room Information**: Details about rooms you create, join, or receive invitations to.
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

### Social Features
- **Friend Lists and Invitations**: Information about users you invite to rooms or designate as room administrators.
- **Chat and Communications**: Messages sent within the app (if applicable).

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain the MiniPlayParty service
- Authenticate users and manage accounts
- Facilitate room creation, invitations, and multiplayer gameplay
- Improve user experience and develop new features
- Send notifications about game updates, invitations, and important service announcements
- Ensure fair play and prevent cheating or abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:
- **Other Players**: Your username, profile information, and game statistics may be visible to other players in shared rooms.
- **Service Providers**: Third-party services that help us operate the app (hosting, analytics, authentication providers).
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access and update your personal information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt-out of promotional communications
- Request a copy of your data

## Children's Privacy

MiniPlayParty is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
