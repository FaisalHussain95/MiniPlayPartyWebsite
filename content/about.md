## Getting Started

1. **Download the App**: Available on iOS App Store and Google Play Store
2. **Create Your Account**: Choose your preferred sign-in method
3. **Complete the Tutorial** (optional): Learn the basics of navigating MiniPlayParty
4. **Join or Create a Room**: Start playing with friends!

## Room Administration

Room creators and designated administrators can:
- Edit room details (name, image)
- Invite new members
- Remove members if necessary
- Promote other members to admin status
